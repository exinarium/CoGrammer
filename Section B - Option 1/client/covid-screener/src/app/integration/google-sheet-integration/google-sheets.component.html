Integrate with Google Sheets

To start pushing your contacts to Google Sheets follow these steps:

  • Click on the Sign in with Google button. Please ensure to allow popups for this site if asked to.
  • Sign in with you Google Account
  • Once successful you will receive an email with the link to your Google Sheet.
  • Only one sheet can be used per organization. Since the Google Sheet is created by your user, feel free to share it with everyone who needs access.
